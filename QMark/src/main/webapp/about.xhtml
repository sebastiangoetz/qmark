About QMark

Problem

Although mobile devices such as smart phones and tablets offer various services and applications for their users, their energy consumption is a problem of top most importance. Many applications are implemented so inefficiently that batteries run out of power within hours, leading to limited uptimes and user satisfaction.

Idea

To support users by finding energy-efficient applications saving the batteries of their devices, QMark provides energy guidance in form of energy labels for mobile apps.

Process

Depending on their genre, apps are profiled w.r.t. their energy consumption for a dedicated set of use cases and afterwards compared to other apps belonging to the same genre and providing similar services.

The best energy-behaving app of a genre is labeled with an A, other apps are ranked relatively, depending on their energy consumption.

As different users may want to use the same applications differently, weights can be defined by the user to consider different use cases of the benchmark more or less intensly in the average energy consumption. Therefore, users can adapt the labels applications are ranked with for their personal needs and interest.
